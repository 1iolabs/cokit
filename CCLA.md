employees to 1io, and to grant 1io the necessary licenses.

## 1. Definitions

**"Contribution"** means any original work of authorship, including any
modifications or additions to an existing work, that is intentionally submitted
by a Designated Employee to 1io for inclusion in COKIT.

**"Corporation"** means the entity that has executed this Agreement.

**"Designated Employee"** means an individual authorized by the Corporation to
make Contributions on its behalf. The initial list of Designated Employees is
set forth in Schedule A.

**"COKIT"** means the COKIT software project, including the `cokit` repository
and all associated crates, tools, and documentation.

## 5. Designated Employees

The Corporation shall maintain an up-to-date list of Designated Employees
authorized to submit Contributions under this Agreement. The initial list is
attached as Schedule A. The Corporation may update the list by notifying 1io
at license@1io.com.

It is the Corporation's responsibility to notify 1io when a Designated
Employee is no longer authorized to make Contributions (e.g., upon termination
of employment). Contributions made by a Designated Employee prior to such
notification remain licensed under this Agreement.

authors to the extent they are inalienable under applicable law.

The Corporation represents that it has obtained, or will obtain prior to each
Contribution, the consent of each Designated Employee to the terms of this
Section to the extent required under applicable law.

Should a Designated Employee wish to submit work that is not the Corporation's
original creation, they may submit it separately from any Contribution,
identifying the complete details of its source and any license or other
restriction of which they are aware, and conspicuously marking the work as
"Submitted on behalf of a third-party: [named here]."
